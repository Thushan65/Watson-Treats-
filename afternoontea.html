Indulge in the quaint charm of Watson Treats, nestled in the heart near Liverpool, where every sip and bite tells a story of elegance and flavor. Our afternoon tea experience is a haven for tea enthusiasts and food connoisseurs alike. Delight in a selection of freshly baked scones, adorned with dollops of heavenly clotted cream and homemade jams. Dive into our delicate finger sandwiches, artfully crafted with premium ingredients, and lose yourself in the symphony of flavors from our assortment of delightful pastries and cakes. At Watson Treats, we elevate the afternoon tea tradition to an art form, offering a cozy ambiance that's perfect for catching up with friends or simply indulging in a moment of blissful solitude. Come, savor the moments with us at Watson Treats, where every sip and nibble whispers tales of pure culinary delight.
